Our seasoned apparatus service technicians lead and perform testing, maintenance and repair of low and medium voltage circuit breakers, transformers, switchgear, motor control and other electrical apparatus in the shop and in the field. Adhering to our CE Power Fundamental- “ Always Safe”, all of our employees contribute to a safe work environment, follow all safety and Human Performance best practices, and work to uphold CE Power Fundamentals.

As a Senior or a Lead Apparatus Service Technician or Field Engineer, you will be responsible for:

  • Performing testing on low and medium voltage breakers including Megger, Ductor, Hi-Pot, Hi-Current, and Timing tests.

  • Painting breakers and parts.
  • Wiring circuit breakers.
  • Utilizing and accurately reading meters.
  • Reading schematics and wiring drawings.
  • Troubleshooting circuit breaker controls.
  • Reading and plotting time-current curves.
  • Testing power and current transformers, including Power Factor and TTR.
  • Accurately and consistently documenting using Power DB.
  • Able to work with electrical tools and socket sets.
